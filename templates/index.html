About Vegetables

Vegetables are parts of plants that are consumed by humans or other animals as food. They include flowers, fruits, stems, leaves, roots, and seeds.

Examples include carrots, broccoli, and spinach. They are essential for a healthy diet due to their high vitamin, mineral, and fiber content.
